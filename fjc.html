Family Justice Centers

Family Justice Centers (FJC) provides clients access to civil, criminal and social services all under one roof. At the FJC, clients are assigned a case worker who is able to help them choose from over 30 community based organizations to meet their individual needs. Clients are free to visit any FJC with or without an appointment, although there might be some waiting time depending on the day and time a client chooses to go.
