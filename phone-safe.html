Stay safe with your phone

Tips to help use your phone more safely

Internet

  • It is very important to clear your browsing history in order to ensure that your partner cannot track it. Depending on what browser and device, there are different ways to clear the history.

    • How to Clear Browsing History on iPhone
    • How to Clear Browsing History on Android

  • Set your internet browser to “Private” mode if you do not want your history to be tracked.

    • How to Set Private Mode on iPhone
    • How to Set Private Mode on Android

Password Security

  • Try to keep your password secret. If your partner knows your password, change it immediately. Do not use personal information to set up your password (ex. birthday, children’s name).

    • How to Set a Secure Password

Socia Media

  • Anything posted on any social media network is viewable by the public and once posted, can be tracked.

  • How to Block Users on Facebook: Log into your account. Click on the privacy icon in the top right hand corner of the page. On the main privacy settings page, you can block specific users from seeing your profile or only allow them to see part of it.

Location Tracking

  • Never leave your phone location services (GPS) turned on if you are worried about being tracked by your partner.

    • How to Turn off GPS on iPhone
